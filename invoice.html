Kindly take note:
1. The payment of a TV licence is a legal obligation.
2. Licence fees are payable on an annual basis.
3. To avoid penalties, please pay the amount due before 1 November of the applicable renewal period.
4. Kindly notify us if there are any problems with this invoice.
5. A licence year means the period from the first day of October to the thirtieth day of September the following year after which the licence must be renewed. Alternatively, the licence holder must immediately dispose of his/her set and notify the NBC in writing.
6. In addition to the relevant licence fee, any person who fails to take out a television licence will be required to pay an amount equal to 10% of the licence fee as a penalty in respect of every month or part thereof during which he/she failed to take out a licence, provided that the penalty does not exceed the licence fee.
7. Any person who in any manner transfers a television set to another person must inform the NBC immediately of the new owner’s name and address and of the date on which the television set changed hands. To avoid a fine, the new owner must take out a licence immediately.
8.
